As you practice touch typing, the keys will glow in a color based on your accuracy. Anything not red is good.

Accuracy Graph
Accuracy graph in top right indicates your overall accuracy percentage. For example, if your accurcy is 73% at a given time, it means out of 100 letters you have typed 73 letters correctly. A visible marker is shown at 90th percentage in the graph.
Speed Graph

Speed Graph is present below accuracy graph. It shows two speeds - Gross Speed and Net Speed.

Gross Speed indicates your total hits (correctly & incorrectly typed letters) per minute.

Net Speed indicates your correct hits (correctly typed letters) per minute.

The graph shows your speed in Words Per Minute (WPM) which is an unit to describe how many average words you would have typed in a minute. A standard average word is of 5 letter length.

Live Statistics
The bottom left box is the live statistics box which gives following information on the keys you have typed so far - Typed Key, Total hits, correct hits and Accuracy. You can pause at any time and take a deep look at this anytime as you practice typing.
Auto Pausing
The application automatically pauses after 8 seconds if you do not type anything during your practice. You can resume your course by pressing space button.
FAQ
The following section describes some of the advanced features of the app.
How to differentiate between Caps and Small keys in the key glow?

Key glows correspond to the most recent key press (i.e glowing the most recently pressed character out of the two characters for the same key in the keyboard). From the key glows, you can visually differentiate capital letters from small letters, numbers from special characters, top row special characters from bottom row special characters in the onscreen keys, thus enabling you to visually identify the accuracy of the right key during the progress of the course.

Capital letters are denoted by a small dash above and below the character. If the most recently pressed key is a capital letter, you can identify with this visual differentiation.

To get the detailed statistics on both the keys, you can pause the application and refer to the live statistics window.

How to differentiate between numbers and special characters in top row?

If the most recently pressed key is a number, you will notice a dash in the top row.

To get the detailed statistics on both the keys, you can pause the application and refer to the live statistics window.

How to differentiate between two special characters in a key?

If the most recently pressed key is a symbol in the top row, you will notice a dash above and below the top special character.

To get the detailed statistics on both the keys, you can pause the application and refer to the live statistics window.
